BIOMECHANICS & HAND CARE

Snatch Walking (Pacing Tool)

Use a light bell (15-20% BW). Snatch overhead, take one step forward. Snatch, step backward. Forces you to lock out, pace yourself, and match your breath biomechanically to the movement.

Tame The Arc (Hand Care)

The biggest limiter is tearing a callus. Keep the bell path vertical like zipping a jacket. Do not "cast" it out like a fishing rod on the drop. Lead with the watch on the way up, punch through the handle to save the skin.
